Climate Justice Advocacy

Climate change affects women and gender-diverse individuals hardest, yet their voices are often ignored and climate adaptive resources do not include their needs. We work with communities to combat climate change through advocacy, education, and sustainable practices, ensuring equity and resilience for all.

  • Research & Documentation – Collect data on how climate change affects LGBTQI+ persons in Nigeria.
  • Policy Engagement – Advocate for climate policies that explicitly recognize and protect SOGIESC rights.
  • Community-Led Solutions – Support queer-inclusive sustainability initiatives.
  • Collaboration – Build alliances between LGBTQI+ movements and environmental justice advocates.
